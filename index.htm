Green Book

The online student notebook

About


Marketed towards college and high school students, Greenbook is the eco-friendly alternative to notebooks. Compact, portable and lightweight, Greenbook is designed to provide users with more organized notes in a simple use of a tablet.


Our Values


MISSION: By providing the user with a more organized and sustainable digital notepad in the simple use of an app, the user will be able to keep all of their notes while less paper will be wasted.


VISION: Our vision is to at one point, have every student use this notebook in order to reduce our carbon footprint in the world. :)
